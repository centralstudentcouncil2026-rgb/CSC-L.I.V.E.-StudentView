About L.I.V.E.

L.I.V.E., which stands for League Information & Viewing Engine, is a gaming information and viewing platform created to improve how players, organizers, and viewers experience gaming events at Adventist University of the Philippines.

The system was published in [insert publication date] and was created by the CSC President of the S.Y. 2026-2027, Rex Dominic F. Perico as a project designed to support and modernize AUP's gaming community. L.I.V.E. serves as a centralized platform where users can view league information, game schedules, team details, match updates, standings, and other important tournament-related content.

The main purpose of L.I.V.E. is to make gaming events more organized, accessible, and engaging. Instead of relying on scattered announcements, manual updates, or incomplete information, the system provides a structured and convenient way for users to stay informed about ongoing and upcoming games.

L.I.V.E. aims to innovate the gaming system of AUP by introducing a more digital, interactive, and efficient approach to managing esports and gaming-related activities. It can help improve communication between organizers and participants, provide easier access to league updates, and create a better viewing experience for students and audiences. Through this system, AUP's gaming environment can become more professional, organized, and connected.
